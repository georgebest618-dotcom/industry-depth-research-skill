# Framework Ledger

## Core Required

| Name | Role | When To Use |
|---|---|---|
| 行研框架篇的主要内容框架 | Overall industry research backbone | Every deep industry report |
| 研究对象和颗粒度 | Boundary-setting before analysis | Before market sizing or competition analysis |
| 商业模式画布 | Basic map of value creation and capture | Before feasibility, scale, moat, and profitability |
| 产业生命周期 | Stage diagnosis and weighting engine | When deciding what matters most |
| 行业研究分析框架：可行性 | Business model viability | New industries, new business models, second curves |
| 行业研究分析框架：规模性 | Market size and growth room | Growth industries, TAM/SAM/SOM, listing potential |
| 行业研究分析框架：防守性 | Moat and substitution resistance | Mature industries, competitive advantage, disruption risk |
| 行业研究分析框架：盈利性 | Who captures profit | Mature or cyclical industries, industry chain analysis |
| 行业研究分析框架：估值 | Price and attractiveness | Investment, fundraising, acquisition, valuation |
| 行业研究分析框架：外部因素 | External drivers and constraints | Policy, macro, technology, social-cultural change |
| 行业研究分析框架：景气度 | Dynamic validation | Tracking near-term performance and inflection points |
| 麦肯锡分析师的“资讯料理法” | Research execution and communication | Turning evidence into a report |
