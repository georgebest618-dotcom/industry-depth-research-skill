# Workflow

Use this workflow to produce an industry depth research report.

## 1. Frame The Assignment

Capture industry or sector name, region, time horizon, user purpose, and output shape. User purposes include investment, startup, market entry, consulting, content, career learning, and strategy. If any field is missing but not decision-critical, make a conservative assumption and label it.

## 2. Define The Research Object

Before analysis, define the industry boundary and granularity: upstream/midstream/downstream scope, included products, substitutes, geography, customer segment, and whether the user means industry, market, niche, or 赛道.

Flag 口径 risk when sources may define the market differently.

## 3. Build The 商业模式画布

Treat `商业模式画布` as required. Use it to establish the base map before deeper judgments: customer segments, value proposition, channels, customer relationships, revenue streams, key resources, key activities, key partners, and cost structure. Add product attributes, pricing power, cash flow, regulation, data, technology dependency, or distribution control when useful.

## 4. Identify Lifecycle Stage

Use `产业生命周期`, improved with penetration logic:
- 导入期: low penetration, uncertain business model, early adopters dominate
- 成长期: penetration rising, demand expands, entrants increase
- 成熟期: growth slows, market stabilizes, share and profitability matter more
- 衰退期: substitution or demand erosion dominates

Then assign module weights: 导入期 emphasizes `行业研究分析框架：可行性`; 成长期 emphasizes `行业研究分析框架：规模性`; 成熟期 emphasizes `行业研究分析框架：防守性` and `行业研究分析框架：盈利性`; 衰退期 emphasizes substitution risk and low-valuation traps.

## 5. Run The Core Modules

### 行业研究分析框架：可行性

Use when testing whether a business model can work. Sales feasibility uses `时间对标法` and `空间对标法`. Profit feasibility uses frequency, demand elasticity, cost standardization, and `UE??`. For innovation industries, check `???????`.

### 行业研究分析框架：规模性

Use when estimating opportunity size. Define TAM, SAM, and SOM. Choose `需求导向` for TAM, `供给导向` for SOM/capacity/unit revenue, and `供需匹配` when supply-demand ratios are stable. Show key assumptions and use optimistic, neutral, and pessimistic cases when assumptions drive the result.

### 行业研究分析框架：防守性

Use when assessing moat and substitution resistance. Check resource monopoly through labor, land, capital, technology, and data. Check network effect through supplier, customer, channel, ecosystem, and relationship control. Ask whether the moat can widen, weaken, or be bypassed.

### 行业研究分析框架：盈利性

Use when judging who makes money. Horizontal structure covers market share, concentration, entry barriers, and price war risk. Vertical structure covers industry chain, bargaining willingness, bargaining power, and margin by link. In mature or cyclical markets, inspect capacity cycle, inventory cycle, and supply-demand balance.

### 行业研究分析框架：估值

Use when the report involves investment, fundraising, acquisition, or market pricing. Investor side: ability, liquidity, and risk appetite. Asset side: odds and probability. Watch `?????` when a sector is popular. Separate good industry from good price.

### 行业研究分析框架：外部因素

Use `PEST???`: Political, Economic, Socio-cultural, and Technological factors. Explain how each factor affects fundamentals and valuation.

### 行业研究分析框架：景气度

Use to validate and update the view. Build a basket of high-frequency indicators, use upstream/downstream signals and industry-specific key metrics, and explain the business logic behind every indicator.

## 6. Process Evidence

Use `麦肯锡分析师的“资讯料理法”`: input with `假设驱动`; process with `金字塔原理` and `MECE??`; output with `SCQR??` only when it fits the audience. Separate facts, hypotheses, and judgments.

## 7. Report Structure

Default report: executive thesis, boundary and assumptions, 商业模式画布, lifecycle judgment, feasibility, scale, moat, profitability, valuation, external factors, prosperity indicators, key risks, disconfirming evidence, and next research steps.
